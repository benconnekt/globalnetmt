Home
  • This module displays the current schedules for all the users in your office
  • User can navigate through other dates by clicking on the required date on the right hand calendar
  • Click on the time column to view the details of the schedule.
Schedules
  • To add a new schedule, click on Schedule a meeting link to add a schedule. Click on Add an appointment to submit the schedule.
  • To view the list of schedules, click on Appointment lists to view the list of appointments for the current month. 
  • Click on the date column in the list to view the details and update them.
  • User can view appointments based on customer and based on month/year
Customers
  • To add a new schedule, click on New customer link to add a new customer to the system. Click on Add customer to submit the details.
  • To view the list of customers, click on Customer lists /Customers Icon to view the list of customers. 
  • Click on New Group link to add a new group to the system. This group is used in grouping the customers and will be chosen at the time of adding customers.
  • Click on the customer name column in the list to view the details and update them.
  • Click on Order Icon to make an order for the customer.

 

Offices
  • To add an office, click on Add office  link to add a new office/agent to the system. Click on Submit Information  to submit the details.
  • To view the list of offices, click on Office lists /Offices Icon to view the list of offices. 
  • Click on the office name column in the list to view the details and update them.
  • The agent prefix is used for all the transactions raised by the users of that office
  • Agent type as collection point will display the offices in the collection dropdown in the order form
Money Transfer
  • To add a new money transfer application, click on New MT Application / Money Transfer icon.
  • Enter customer details, beneficiary details including beneficiary amount and click on Calculate to get the beneficiary amount and exchange rate.
  • Choose the collection point, specify additional information and click on Create new sale to submit the application
  • Transaction code will be displayed for the user. 
  • Click on Printable view to print the receipt for the application
Money Transfer Reports
  • Money Transfer sales: Displays the list of money transfer sales of the office the user logged in
  • Collections: Displays the list of collections of the office the user logged in
  • MT Daily Sales:  Displays the list of MT sales on a daily basis and on an office basis. Choose the date and the office to view the respective sales
  • MT Agent Sales: Displays the list of MT sales on a monthly basis and on an office basis. Choose the month and the office to view the respective sales
  • Collection by Agent: Displays the list of MT Collections  on a monthly basis and on an office basis. Choose the month and the office to view the respective collections
  • MT Sales by Customer: Displays the list of MT Collections  by customer. Choose the customer to view the respective sales

Click on  printable view to print the report
Click on Export to excel to export the report to a csv

Accounts
  • Click on Add Payment/T.T to record payments made to the suppliers. Click on Add Payment button to submit the information
  • Click on Payments/T.T's to view the list of payments made based on each month
  • To view/update the details of the payment, click on the date column to view/update the details
  • Click on Sales Report link to view the list of sales and payments made between specific dates. Click on printable format to print the report
  • Click on Setup Rates to setup exchange rates and other company information in the system
System
  • Click on New User link to add a new user to the system.
  • Assign office, rights and other details to the user which will be applied through out the system.
  • Administrator you has system rights can update the users and their permissions. 
  • Click on New Country link to add a new country to the system.
  • Administrator you has system rights can update the users and their permissions.
